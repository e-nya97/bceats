About

BC Eats is a non-profit organization that aims to integrate people with the farming community of British Columbia through educational experiences, such as workshops and farm visits; to ensure the longevity of the agricultural industry.

Mission

To educate and connect people with BC’s agricultural industry and farmers by offering seminars, conferences and meetings.

Vision

To educate youths: by ensuring the longevity of the agricultural industry through hands-on experiences.

Values

Maintainting BC’s ecological and agricultural environment by building relationships with local farmers, schools, volunteers  and partners.
